Gage Sommerville Light Opera

The Pirates of Penzance

Act 1 | Scene: A rocky seashore on the coast of Cornwall. In the distance is a calm sea, on which a schooner is lying at anchor. Rock L. sloping down to L.C. of stage. Under these rocks is a cavern, the entrance to which is seen at first entrance L. A natural arch of rock occupies the R.C. of the stage. FREDERIC is seated in a despondent attitude at the back of the scene. RUTH kneels at his feet.

            ALL:    Pour, O pour the pirate sherry;
                        Fill, O fill the pirate glass;
                      And,to make us more than merry
                           Let the pirate bumper pass.

            SAMUEL: For today our pirate 'prentice
                      Rises from indentures freed;
                      Strong his arm, and keen his scent is
                      He's a pirate now indeed!

            ALL:      Here's good luck to Fred'ric's ventures!
                      Fred'ric's out of his indentures.

            SAMUEL:             
                      Two and twenty, now he's rising,
                      And alone he's fit to fly,
                      Which we're bent on signalizing
                      With unusual revelry.

            ALL:                
                      Here's good luck to Fred'ric's ventures!
                      Fred'ric's out of his indentures.
                      Pour, O pour the pirate sherry;
                      Fill, O fill the pirate glass;
                      And, to make us more than merry
                      Let the pirate bumper pass.

            (FREDERIC rises and comes forward with PIRATE KING, who enters)

            KING:     
                      Yes, Frederic, from to-day you rank as a full-blown
                      member of our band.

            ALL:      Hurrah!

            FREDERIC:    
                      My friends, I thank you all, from my heart, for your
                      kindly wishes.  Would that I could repay them as they
                      deserve!
